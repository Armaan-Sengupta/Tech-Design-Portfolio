Presenting Ideas

Although orthographics are a popular way of expressing your design, I much prefer animations. No doubt, they take longer to make and are harder to make. However, for the person watching your work, a video animation is much more helpful than a stagnant mess of dotted lines. Animations can also have narration, which further helps to present the idea. Hence I create animations to try and describe my ideas. The most notable instance of this was during the optics project when I created an animation for every design and version. Since that is a lot, I will show you the final video and link you to the original drawings and animation for the other models.

This is the final video made for the project. It is almost 90% animations of the product, sometimes guided with images. I believe this video is very effective in the presentation of my ideas and beliefs.
I created a video, but I also made an entire website where you can buy each component of the product or packs of products. The site helps realize how the product would be sold while still having the ability to contain plenty of images, animations, descriptions, and even the video seen above. To access, the website click here: https://senguptaarmaan.wixsite.com/optica-technica. The video, coupled with the website, made this an excellent presentation and demonstration of what the product is, how it works, and where it will be sold.
